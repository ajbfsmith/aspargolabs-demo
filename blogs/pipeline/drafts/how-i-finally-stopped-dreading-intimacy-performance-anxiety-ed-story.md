## A Note on Safety and Health

While my journey led me to a solution that worked for my lifestyle, ED is a complex medical condition. It is often a "canary in the coal mine" for other health issues. According to the [Mayo Clinic](https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/erectile-dysfunction/symptoms-causes/syc-20355776), ED can be an early sign of heart disease, diabetes, or high blood pressure.

Before starting any ED medication, including HEZKUE, you must consult with a healthcare provider. This is especially critical because:

- **Nitrates:** You must never take sildenafil if you are taking nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or pulmonary hypertension medications, as this can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** If you have unstable heart disease or recent history of stroke, you must be cleared by a doctor.
- **Serious Symptoms:** Seek immediate medical attention if you experience chest pain, sudden vision or hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism).
