## Safety and Cardiovascular Health

Safety is the primary concern when discussing **hezkue older men age**. Because the prevalence of cardiovascular issues increases with age, it is vital to approach ED treatment with medical oversight.

**Important Safety Warning:** Sildenafil (HEZKUE) must never be taken by men who use organic nitrates (such as nitroglycerin for chest pain) or pulmonary arterial hypertension medications like riociguat. The combination can cause a dangerous, life-threatening drop in blood pressure.

Older men should also be aware of the following:
- **Cardiovascular Risk:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease or a recent history of heart attack or stroke must consult a clinician before using HEZKUE.
