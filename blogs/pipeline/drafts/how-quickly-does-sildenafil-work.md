## Factors That Influence Onset Time

While the 60-minute mark is the standard clinical benchmark, several variables can change how **quickly sildenafil work?** in a real-world setting.

### Delivery Format
Standard tablets must be broken down in the stomach and processed through the digestive system before entering the bloodstream. This process naturally creates a "wait time." In contrast, clinically formulated oral spray suspensions, such as HEZKUE, bypass some of these digestive hurdles. ### Diet and Food Intake
What you eat plays a significant role in absorption. Taking sildenafil on an empty stomach generally leads to the fastest onset. If you take a tablet after a high-fat meal (such as a steak or a burger), the absorption can be delayed by an hour or more. This is because the body prioritizes processing the fats, which slows down the rate at which the medication reaches the small intestine for absorption.

## How Long Does the Effect Last?
Once sildenafil begins working, it typically remains effective for about four to five hours. This does not mean you will have a continuous erection for that duration; rather, it means your body’s ability to achieve an erection in response to stimulation is enhanced during this window. ## Safety and Medical Precautions
Sildenafil is a potent medication that affects the cardiovascular system. It is not safe for everyone, and a consultation with a healthcare provider is essential before use.

**Important Safety Warnings:**
- **Nitrates:** Sildenafil is strictly contraindicated for men taking nitrates (such as nitroglycerin or isosorbide) for chest pain or heart conditions. Combining these can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease or recent heart events must speak with a clinician before use.
- **Urgent Care:** Seek immediate medical attention if you experience chest pain, sudden vision loss, sudden hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism).
