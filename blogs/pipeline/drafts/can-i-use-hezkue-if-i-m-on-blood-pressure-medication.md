## How Sildenafil Interacts with Blood Pressure

Sildenafil belongs to a class of drugs called PDE5 inhibitors. These medications work by relaxing the smooth muscles in the blood vessels, allowing for increased blood flow to specific areas of the body. While this is effective for treating ED, it also causes a mild, temporary systemic drop in blood pressure.

According to the [American Heart Association](https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/erectile-dysfunction/erectile-dysfunction-and-hypertension), ED and high blood pressure often go hand-in-hand because both are related to vascular health. When you combine a PDE5 inhibitor like HEZKUE with certain antihypertensive drugs, the blood-pressure-lowering effects can be additive. For most men on standard blood pressure medications, this drop is not clinically significant, but for others, it can lead to dizziness or fainting.

## The Absolute Contraindication: Nitrates

The most critical safety rule regarding HEZKUE and blood pressure involves nitrates. If you take nitrates for chest pain (angina) or heart problems, you cannot use HEZKUE.

Nitrates—such as nitroglycerin, isosorbide mononitrate, or isosorbide dinitrate—are potent vasodilators. When taken with sildenafil, they can cause a sudden and life-threatening drop in blood pressure (hypotension). This warning also applies to recreational "poppers" (amyl nitrate).

If you experience chest pain after taking HEZKUE, you must seek emergency medical care immediately. Do not take nitrates to treat the chest pain, as the interaction can be fatal.

## Using HEZKUE with Other Blood Pressure Medications

If you are on other types of blood pressure medications, the compatibility of HEZKUE depends on the drug class:

- **Alpha-Blockers:** Medications like tamsulosin or doxazosin (often prescribed for both high blood pressure and enlarged prostate) can interact with sildenafil. To minimize the risk of low blood pressure, patients should be on a stable dose of their alpha-blocker before starting HEZKUE at the lowest recommended dose.
- **ACE Inhibitors and Calcium Channel Blockers:** Generally, sildenafil can be used alongside these medications, but a doctor must evaluate your specific dosage and health history.
- **Diuretics:** These are commonly used for hypertension and typically do not have a direct contraindication with sildenafil, though dehydration can exacerbate blood pressure fluctuations.

Before your **first time using hezkue**, ensure your prescribing clinician has a full list of every medication and supplement you take.

## When to Seek Medical Attention

Safety is the priority when treating ED alongside hypertension. You should speak with a clinician if you have unstable cardiovascular disease or serious symptoms like shortness of breath.

Seek urgent medical care if you experience:
- Chest pain or symptoms of a heart attack.
- A painful erection or one that lasts longer than 4 hours (priapism).
- Sudden vision loss in one or both eyes.
- Sudden hearing loss or dizziness.
