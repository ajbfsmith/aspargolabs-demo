## Step-by-Step HEZKUE Dosing Guide

To get the most out of HEZKUE, the application process must be consistent. Improper use, such as failing to shake the bottle, can result in an inconsistent dose.

### 1. Shake the Bottle Thoroughly
Because HEZKUE is a suspension, the active sildenafil can settle at the bottom of the canister over time. Before every use, shake the bottle vigorously for several seconds. This ensures the medication is evenly distributed throughout the liquid, providing the exact milligram dosage your clinician prescribed.

### 2. Prime the Pump
If you are using a new bottle for the first time, or if you haven't used your HEZKUE spray in several days, you must "prime" the pump. Point the nozzle away from your face and press the actuator 2–3 times until a fine, consistent mist is released. This ensures the internal mechanism is filled with the medication rather than air.

### 3. Direct Application
Open your mouth and direct the nozzle toward the inside of your cheek or under your tongue, as directed by your healthcare provider. Press the pump firmly to release the metered dose. 

### 4. Wait Before Swallowing
While HEZKUE is designed for oral delivery, allowing the mist to coat the oral mucosa (the lining of the mouth) for a few seconds before swallowing can assist in the delivery process. Unlike pills that must pass through the entire digestive tract, oral sprays begin the absorption process much sooner.

## Safety and Contraindications

Before using HEZKUE, it is vital to understand that sildenafil is a potent vasodilator. It is not suitable for everyone, and certain medical conditions or drug interactions can be life-threatening.

**Important Safety Warning:**
You must never take HEZKUE if you use nitrates (such as nitroglycerin) for chest pain or heart problems. Combining sildenafil with nitrates can cause a sudden, dangerous drop in blood pressure. This also includes "poppers" (amyl nitrate or nitrite) used recreationally.

Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, a recent history of stroke or heart attack, or severe liver or kidney impairment must consult a clinician before use. ### When to Seek Urgent Medical Care
Stop using HEZKUE and seek immediate medical attention if you experience:
- Chest pain, dizziness, or nausea during sexual activity.
- A prolonged erection lasting more than 4 hours (priapism), which can lead to permanent tissue damage.
- Sudden loss of vision in one or both eyes.
- Sudden decrease or loss of hearing.
