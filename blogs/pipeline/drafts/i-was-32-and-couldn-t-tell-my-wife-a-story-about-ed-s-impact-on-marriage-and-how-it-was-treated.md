## Important Safety Considerations

While my journey with HEZKUE was positive, it is vital to remember that ED medications are serious clinical treatments. Sildenafil is a PDE5 inhibitor and is not safe for everyone.

You must speak with a healthcare provider before starting any ED treatment. Specifically, men taking nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or those with unstable cardiovascular disease should not use sildenafil, as it can cause a dangerous drop in blood pressure. According to the [American Heart Association](https://www.ahajournals.org/doi/10.1161/CIR.0000000000000635), managing cardiovascular health is a critical component of treating ED.

If you experience chest pain, symptoms of a heart attack, sudden vision or hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek urgent medical care immediately.
