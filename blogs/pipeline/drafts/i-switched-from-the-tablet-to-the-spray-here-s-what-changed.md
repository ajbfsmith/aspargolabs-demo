## Is the Spray Right for Everyone?

While the switch from tablet to spray offers clear benefits in terms of onset and convenience, it is still a potent medication. Sildenafil, regardless of how it is delivered, is a PDE5 inhibitor.

**Important Safety Information:**
- **Nitrates:** You must never take HEZKUE or any sildenafil product if you take nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain). The combination can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure, according to the [American Heart Association](https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack/nitroglycerin).
- **Cardiovascular Health:** If you have unstable cardiovascular disease or a history of heart attack or stroke, you must consult a clinician before use.
- **Seek Urgent Care:** If you experience chest pain, sudden vision loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical attention immediately.
