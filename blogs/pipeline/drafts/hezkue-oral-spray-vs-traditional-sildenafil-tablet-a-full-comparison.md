## Delivery Innovation: Spray vs. Tablet

The primary difference between HEZKUE oral spray and traditional sildenafil is the delivery format. Traditional tablets are solid oral dosage forms that require "disintegration" and "dissolution" in the stomach before the medication can be absorbed into the bloodstream. According to the [FDA-approved labeling for sildenafil tablets](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/drugsatfda_docs/label/2014/203109s000lbl.pdf), this process typically results in a peak plasma concentration about 60 minutes after ingestion.

In contrast, HEZKUE is an oral spray suspension. This format allows the medication to be applied directly to the oral mucosa. Because the sildenafil is already in a liquid suspension, it does not need to be broken down like a hard pill. This delivery innovation aims to provide a more streamlined path to absorption. ## Absorption and Consistency

One of the challenges with traditional sildenafil tablets is the "food effect." Taking a tablet with a high-fat meal can delay absorption by approximately 60 minutes and reduce the peak concentration in the blood. This can lead to unpredictable results, making it difficult for men to time their medication.

HEZKUE oral spray is designed to mitigate some of these variables. By utilizing a spray suspension, the medication may be absorbed more consistently regardless of recent food intake. ## Safety and Important Precautions

While the delivery format changes, the underlying medication—sildenafil—remains a potent PDE5 inhibitor. It is not a miracle cure and is not suitable for everyone.

**Contraindications and Warnings:**
- **Nitrates:** You must never take HEZKUE or any sildenafil product if you take nitrates (such as nitroglycerin) for chest pain or heart problems. The combination can cause a sudden, life-threatening drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, recent heart attack, or stroke must consult a clinician before use.
- **Serious Side Effects:** Seek urgent medical care if you experience chest pain, sudden vision loss, or sudden hearing loss.
- **Priapism:** If an erection lasts longer than four hours, seek immediate medical attention to avoid permanent tissue damage.

Research published in [The Journal of Sexual Medicine](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27914485/) indicates that oral spray sildenafil is generally well-tolerated, with a safety profile similar to that of the tablet form, but a professional medical consultation is always required to ensure it is safe for your specific health profile.
