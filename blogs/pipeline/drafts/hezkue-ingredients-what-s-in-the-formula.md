## Safety and Contraindications

Regardless of the delivery format, sildenafil is a potent medication that requires clinical oversight. The HEZKUE formula is highly effective for many, but it is not safe for everyone.

### The Nitrate Warning
You must never use HEZKUE if you are taking nitrates for chest pain or heart problems. This includes medications like nitroglycerin, isosorbide dinitrate, and isosorbide mononitrate, as well as recreational "poppers" containing amyl nitrite. Combining PDE5 inhibitors with nitrates can cause a sudden, life-threatening drop in blood pressure, according to the [American Heart Association](https://www.ahajournals.org/doi/10.1161/01.cir.99.1.168).

### Cardiovascular Health
Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, a recent history of stroke or heart attack, or severe liver or kidney impairment should consult a doctor before using any sildenafil product. ### When to Seek Urgent Care
Stop using the product and seek immediate medical attention if you experience:
- Chest pain, dizziness, or nausea during sexual activity.
- A prolonged erection lasting more than four hours (priapism), which can cause permanent damage.
- Sudden loss of vision in one or both eyes.
- Sudden decrease or loss of hearing.
