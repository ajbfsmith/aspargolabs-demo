Choosing the right erectile dysfunction (ED) medication depends on your lifestyle and sexual health goals. The primary difference between sildenafil and tadalafil is their duration of action: **sildenafil is a short-acting medication that lasts for 4–6 hours, while tadalafil is a long-acting option that remains effective for up to 36 hours.** Sildenafil is typically used for planned sexual activity, whereas tadalafil allows for more spontaneity or can be taken as a daily low-dose treatment.

Both medications belong to a class of drugs called PDE5 inhibitors, which improve blood flow to the penis. While they share a similar mechanism, understanding their differences in onset, food interactions, and side effects is essential for finding the right fit.

## Sildenafil: The On-Demand Standard

Sildenafil, the active ingredient in Viagra, is the most well-known ED treatment. It is designed for occasional, planned use.

- **Timing:** It is generally taken 30 to 60 minutes before sexual activity.
- **Duration:** The effects typically last for [4 to 6 hours](https://www.mayoclinic.org/drugs-supplements/sildenafil-oral-route/description/drg-20066904).
- **Food Interactions:** Sildenafil's absorption can be delayed by a high-fat meal. If taken after a heavy dinner, it may take significantly longer to become effective.

For many men, the shorter duration is preferable because the medication leaves the system relatively quickly. You can learn more about how this compares to other options in our guide to [sildenafil tadalafil vardenafil avanafil](/blog/sildenafil-tadalafil-vardenafil-avanafil-comparison).

## Tadalafil: The "Weekend Pill"

Tadalafil, the active ingredient in Cialis, is unique because of its long half-life. It is often called the "weekend pill" because a single dose can remain effective for up to 36 hours.

- **Timing:** Like sildenafil, it can be taken on-demand about 30 to 60 minutes before sex. However, it is also available in a low-dose daily version (2.5 mg or 5 mg) that maintains a steady level of the drug in your system, allowing for spontaneity at any time.
- **Duration:** Up to [36 hours](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/drugs/20268-tadalafil-tablets).
- **Food Interactions:** Tadalafil is not significantly affected by food, meaning it can be taken with or without a meal.

## Comparing Side Effects and Considerations

While both drugs are generally well-tolerated, they have slightly different side-effect profiles. Both can cause headache, flushing, and indigestion. However:

- **Sildenafil** is more likely to cause temporary visual changes, such as a blue tinge to vision or sensitivity to light.
- **Tadalafil** is more likely to cause muscle aches or back pain, which can last for a day or two after the dose.

## Important Safety Information

PDE5 inhibitors are not safe for everyone. **You must never take sildenafil or tadalafil if you take nitrates** (such as nitroglycerin for chest pain) or certain other medications, as this can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.

Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, severe heart failure, or a history of recent stroke or heart attack must speak with a clinician before using any ED treatment. Seek urgent medical care if you experience:

- Chest pain during or after sexual activity.
- An erection lasting more than four hours (priapism).
- Sudden vision loss in one or both eyes.
- Sudden hearing loss.
