## How Does HEZKUE Work?

To understand if HEZKUE will work for you, it helps to understand the biological mechanism of an erection. During sexual stimulation, the body releases nitric oxide, which triggers the production of cGMP. This chemical relaxes the smooth muscles in the penis, allowing blood to flow in. PDE5 is an enzyme that breaks down cGMP; by inhibiting this enzyme, sildenafil helps maintain higher levels of cGMP, supporting an erection.

## Safety and Medical Considerations

While HEZKUE is a sophisticated delivery innovation, it is still a potent medication. It is not safe for everyone, and a consultation with a healthcare provider is mandatory.

**Important Safety Warnings:**
- **Nitrates:** You must never take HEZKUE if you use nitrate medications for chest pain (nitroglycerin) or pulmonary hypertension. The combination can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure ([American Heart Association](https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack/nitroglycerin-and-erectile-dysfunction-drugs)).
- **Cardiovascular Health:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, recent heart attack, or stroke should consult a specialist before using any PDE5 inhibitor.
- **Priapism:** If you experience an erection lasting longer than four hours, seek emergency medical care immediately to prevent permanent tissue damage.
- **Vision/Hearing:** Stop use and seek urgent care if you experience sudden vision loss or hearing loss.
