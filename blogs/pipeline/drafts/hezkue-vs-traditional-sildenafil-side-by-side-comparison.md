## The Impact of Food

A significant drawback of traditional sildenafil tablets is the "food effect." Taking a sildenafil pill after a high-fat meal (like a steak dinner or pizza) can significantly delay its absorption. The [U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/drugsatfda_docs/label/2014/020895s039lbl.pdf) notes that a high-fat meal can delay the time to peak concentration by about 60 minutes and reduce the peak concentration itself.

HEZKUE’s oral spray format may offer more consistency. Because the spray begins its journey in the mouth and is designed for rapid transit, it is often less susceptible to the significant delays caused by a heavy stomach compared to a dense tablet that must compete with food for digestion.

## Safety and Medical Considerations

Regardless of the delivery format, sildenafil is a potent medication that must be used under clinical supervision. This is true whether you are dealing with [erectile dysfunction by age](/blog/ed-causes-by-age-20s-30s-40s-50s-60s) in your 30s or your 60s.

**Important Safety Warning:**
Sildenafil (in any form) must never be taken by men who use nitrates for chest pain or heart problems. According to the [American Heart Association](https://www.heart.org/en/health-topics/heart-attack/treatment-of-a-heart-attack/nitroglycerin), the combination of sildenafil and nitrates can cause a sudden, life-threatening drop in blood pressure.

You should consult a healthcare provider before using HEZKUE or traditional sildenafil if you have:
- Unstable cardiovascular disease or a recent heart attack/stroke.
- Severe liver or kidney impairment.
- Conditions like retinitis pigmentosa.

Seek emergency medical care immediately if you experience chest pain, sudden vision or hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism).
