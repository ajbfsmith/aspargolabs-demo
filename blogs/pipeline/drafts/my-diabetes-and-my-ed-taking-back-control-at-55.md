### Important Safety Information

Before starting any ED treatment, you must speak with a healthcare provider. This is especially critical for men with diabetes, as they often have co-occurring cardiovascular issues.

- **Nitrates:** Never take sildenafil (HEZKUE) if you take nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or pulmonary hypertension medications, as this can cause a dangerous drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** If you have unstable cardiovascular disease or have recently suffered a heart attack or stroke, consult your cardiologist first.
- **Seek Urgent Care:** If you experience chest pain, sudden vision loss, sudden hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical attention immediately.
