## Identifying Legitimate Platforms vs. Questionable Sources

As the demand for online care grows, so does the presence of unregulated entities. Distinguishing between a legitimate medical platform and a "pill mill" is essential for your safety.

### Signs of a Legitimate Platform:
- Requires a consultation with a licensed medical provider.
- Requires a prescription for PDE5 inhibitors.
- Provides clear information on the risks and side effects of medications.
- Uses pharmacies licensed in the United States.

## Safety and Cardiovascular Considerations

Before seeking any ED treatment, it is important to understand that sexual activity itself can place stress on the heart. Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, recent history of stroke or heart attack, or severe heart failure should consult an in-person specialist before starting treatment.

**Important Safety Warning:**
- **Nitrates:** Never take PDE5 inhibitors if you use nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or "poppers" (amyl nitrate). The combination can lead to a fatal drop in blood pressure.
- **Emergency Symptoms:** Seek immediate medical attention if you experience chest pain during sex, sudden vision loss, sudden hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism).
