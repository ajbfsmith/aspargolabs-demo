## Safety and precautions

HEZKUE is a prescription medication and must be used under medical supervision. It is not suitable for everyone, and certain health conditions or drug interactions can be dangerous.

> **Important Safety Warning:** Sildenafil, the active ingredient in HEZKUE, must never be taken with nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or guanylate cyclase stimulators. This combination can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.

Before you **use Hezkue oral spray**, consult a clinician if you have:
- A history of unstable cardiovascular disease or recent heart attack/stroke.
- Severe liver or kidney impairment.
- Conditions that affect the shape of the penis (such as Peyronie's disease).
- A history of vision loss or "eye strokes."

If you experience sudden chest pain, dizziness, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical care immediately.
