## Safety and Contraindications

It is vital to remember that HEZKUE is a prescription medication and is not safe for everyone. You must speak with a clinician before using HEZKUE, especially if you have underlying health conditions.

**Important Safety Warnings:**
- **Nitrates:** Never take HEZKUE if you are taking nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or pulmonary hypertension medications. The combination can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, recent heart attack, or stroke should not use PDE5 inhibitors without strict medical clearance.
- **Seek Urgent Care:** If you experience chest pain, symptoms of a heart attack, sudden vision loss, sudden hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical attention immediately.
