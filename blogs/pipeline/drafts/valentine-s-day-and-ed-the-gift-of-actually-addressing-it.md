## The Psychological Weight of Valentine's Day ED

The "holiday effect" on sexual health is a documented phenomenon. When intimacy is scheduled or expected, the body’s sympathetic nervous system—the "fight or flight" response—can take over. ## Important Safety Considerations

While modern treatments are highly effective, they are not suitable for everyone. It is critical to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new ED medication.

> **Safety Warning:** PDE5 inhibitors, including sildenafil, are strictly contraindicated for men taking nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or guanylate cyclase stimulators. This combination can lead to a dangerous, life-threatening drop in blood pressure.

Men with unstable cardiovascular disease, a recent history of heart attack or stroke, or severe liver or kidney impairment must speak with a clinician to determine if ED treatments are safe for them. If you experience symptoms such as chest pain during sex, a prolonged erection lasting more than four hours (priapism), or sudden vision or hearing loss, seek emergency medical care immediately.
