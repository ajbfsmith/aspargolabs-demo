## Important Safety Information

While ED medications are highly effective for many, they are not suitable for everyone. It is essential to consult with a healthcare provider before **my first time** trying any PDE5 inhibitor.

- **Nitrates:** You must not take sildenafil if you use nitrate medications for chest pain or heart problems, as this can cause a sudden, dangerous drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Health:** Men with unstable cardiovascular disease or recent heart attack/stroke must be cleared by a clinician.
- **Seek Urgent Care:** If you experience chest pain, sudden vision loss, sudden hearing loss, or an erection lasting longer than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical attention immediately.

According to the [FDA](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/drugsatfda_docs/label/2014/020310s044lbl.pdf), providing a full medical history to your prescriber is the only way to ensure the medication is safe for your specific health profile.
