## A Note on Safety and Cardiovascular Health

If you are experiencing ED after a viral illness, it is vital to speak with a healthcare provider. While my journey involved sildenafil, these medications are not safe for everyone.

**Important Safety Information:**
- **Nitrates:** Never take HEZKUE or any PDE5 inhibitor if you take nitrates (often prescribed for chest pain) or use "poppers" (amyl nitrate). This combination can cause a life-threatening drop in blood pressure.
- **Cardiovascular Risk:** If you have unstable heart disease or a history of recent stroke or heart attack, consult your cardiologist before seeking ED treatment.
