## Who Should Avoid HEZKUE?

Safety is the primary priority when considering any ED medication. HEZKUE is a potent clinical formulation and is not suitable for everyone.

**You must not take HEZKUE if you:**
- **Take Nitrates:** Sildenafil can cause a severe and potentially fatal drop in blood pressure when used with nitrate medications (often prescribed for chest pain or heart conditions) or "poppers" (amyl nitrate).
- **Have Severe Cardiovascular Issues:** Men with unstable angina, recent heart attack, or severe heart failure should not use PDE5 inhibitors, as sexual activity itself can put a strain on the heart.
- **Have Certain Medical Conditions:** Men with severe liver impairment, end-stage kidney disease, or certain inherited eye diseases (like retinitis pigmentosa) must consult a specialist before use.

Always disclose your full medical history and current medication list to a healthcare provider. If you experience chest pain, dizziness, or nausea during sex, or if you experience a prolonged erection lasting more than four hours (priapism), seek emergency medical care immediately.
